--- notes: | Paragraphs of text should always remain at a comfortable width. Too short and the users eyes will jitter, too long and a user may skip lines. Generally speaking a comfortable column width for paragraphs is between 45 to 75 characters including spaces. Line-height and bottom margin should be 1.618x (golden ratio) the size of the font. ---
