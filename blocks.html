Blocks

Buttons

Alert

Usage: Destructive or negative actions.

Examples: Delete, remove.

<button class="btn btn-alert">Alert Button</button>

Neutral

Usage: Neutral actions.

Examples: Cancel, go back.

<button class="btn btn-neutral">Neutral Button</button>

Primary

Usage: Forward action indicator in a critical flow.

Examples: Log in, confirm purchase, connect a device.

<button class="btn btn-primary">Primary Button</button>

Secondary

Usage: Client to server operations not associated with a critical flow. These buttons are positive in nature.

Examples: Refresh data, spawn a modal window.

<button class="btn btn-secondary">Secondary Button</button>

Paragraph

Paragraphs of text should always remain at a comfortable width. Too short and the users eyes will jitter, too long and a user may skip lines. Generally speaking a comfortable column width for paragraphs is between 45 to 75 characters including spaces. Line-height and bottom margin should be 1.618x (golden ratio) the size of the font.
