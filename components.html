Note: These are intended to drop down from the top of the site and remain fixed until dismissed.

Usage: Instances where we need to display more than one notification to the user.

  • Error: There has been a serious problem, or the user has completed a negative action, e.g: burning a device.
  • Info: We just need to surface a neutral notification to the user.
  • Success: User has completed a positive action, e.g: Adding a device or connection.
